# Channel Style Guide

## Amazon
- Prioritize search relevance + clarity.
- Keep claim language conservative and evidence-based.
- Favor concise bullets and scannable features.

## Taobao
- Prioritize storytelling and emotional purchase drivers.
- Use richer visual-text rhythm for detail sections.
- Include stronger promotional and lifestyle context.

## 1688
- Prioritize factory credibility and transaction efficiency.
- Emphasize MOQ, lead time, customization, production capability.
- Keep terms practical for B2B buyers.
